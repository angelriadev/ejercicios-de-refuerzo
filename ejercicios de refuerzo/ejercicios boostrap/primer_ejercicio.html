The beet is the most intense of vegetables. The radish, admittedly, is more feverish, but the fire of the radish is a cold fire, the fire of discontent not of passion. Tomatoes are lusty enough, yet there runs through a tomatoe the undercurrent of frivolity. Beets are deadly serious.

Tom Robbins
